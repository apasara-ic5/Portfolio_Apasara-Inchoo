Palm data

Raw level

Palm oil bunch has orange-red or purplish-red skin and the mesocarp of palm oil fruit is light yellow. No fruit has fallen off

Ripe level

Palm oil bunch has orange-red or purplish-red skin and the mesocarp of palm oil fruit is yellow-orange. In each bunch, it has less than 10 fallen fruits

Over ripe level

Palm oil bunch has orange or red skin and the mesocarp of palm fruit is yellow-orange.In each bunch, it has less than 50% of oil palm fruits fallen
